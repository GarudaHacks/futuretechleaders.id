Frequently Asked Questions

Are these positions paid?
Yes. All of the positions, both interns and fresh graduates, are paid.
Do I need to do the program full-time?
Yes. We only accept candidates who plan to work or intern full-time.
Can I apply to both programs?
Yes. As long as you meet the qualification for both programs, you can apply to both of them. However, you will only be able participate in one.
Can I choose which companies I want to apply to?
Yes. You can choose which companies you are interested in after the screening phase. You can apply to as many positions and companies as you like.
What skills are needed to apply?
The skill requirement for each positons differ from one company to another. Once you have passed our screening, you will be able to select positions that are suitable to your skill.

For Engineering Fellowship candidates, prior programming experience will be needed.
How does Future Tech Leaders Fellowship differ than a typical recruiting program?
Beyond the job, you will be able to participate in our exclusive events to learn and network with industry leaders. You will also be a part of a community of other talented individuals with access to project funding and recruiting opportunities.
