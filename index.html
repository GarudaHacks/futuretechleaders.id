About The
Program

The Future Tech Leaders Fellowship is a selective program designed to prepare the next generation of Indonesia’s technology leaders.

Program Duration: May 2021 - August 2021

Learn from industry leaders

Learn from the executives, founders, and investors that is creating an impact in Indonesia’s technology industry through our weekly exclusive learning event.

Work at leading tech companies

Find out what it is like to build a technology company by working as a paid intern at our partner company where you will make real impact from day 1.

Build lasting connections

Grow your professional network by being a part of our community of like minded individuals and technology industry leaders in Indonesia.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a paid internship?
Yes. We believe that a paid internship will allow our fellows to make the best use of the program by commiting full-time on it.
Do I need to do the program full-time?
Yes. We only accept candidates who plan to work full-time during the period of the program.
What skills are needed to apply?
The skill requirement for each roles differ from one company to another. You can view the list of all positions and its requirement here.
Can I apply to both programs?
Yes. As long as you meet the qualification for both programs, you can apply to both of them. However, you will only be able participate in one.
Can I choose which companies I want to apply to?
Yes. You can choose which companies you are interested in during the screening phase. You can apply to as many companies as you like.
How does Future Tech Leaders Fellowship differ than a typical internship program?
Beyond the internship, you will be able to participate in our exclusive events to learn and network with industry leaders. You will also be a part of a community of other talented individuals with access to project funding and recruiting opportunities.
